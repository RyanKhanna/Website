About

Welcome to Clayton Elementry School! We provide an advanced course for young learners anywhere from kindergarten to 5th grade, while providing a unique learning experince. We have been rated 5 stars by many famlies, and we have also been nominated a school board award for 'Exelent Teaching.' Founded by Nan Clayton in 2007, we strive for child to have the best experience there is.
